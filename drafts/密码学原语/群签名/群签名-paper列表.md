+ [Group signatures (**EUROCRYPT 1991**)](chaum1991group)
最早提出群签名概念并给出定义

+ [Identity escrow (**CRYPTO 1998**)](kilian1998identity)
提出了身份托管概念并指出群签名目前能够实现身份托管的大部分特性。

+ [A group signature scheme with improved efficiency (**ASIACRYPT 1998**)](camenisch1998group)
大规模高效群签名方案，即群体的公钥和签名的长度不依赖于群体的规模。

+ [Separability and efficiency for generic group signature schemes (**CRYPTO 1999**)](camenisch1999separability)
可分离的高效群签名方案，群成员密钥可以独立生成的同时群体的公钥和签名的长度不依赖于群体的规模

+ [A practical and provably secure coalition-resistant group signature scheme (**CRYPTO 2000**)](ateniese2000practical)
抗共谋的群签名，即使所有群成员联合起来，也无法生成群管理员无法open的有效签名。但是此方案缺乏形式化的安全定义，对匿名性的定义过于模糊

+ [Foundations of group signatures: Formal definitions, simplified requirements, and a construction based on general assumptions (**EUROCRYPT 2003**)](bellare2003foundations)

[Multi-signatures for ad-hoc and privacy-preserving group signing (**PKC 2024**)](lehmann2024multi)

[Membership privacy for fully dynamic group signatures (**CCS 2019**)](backes2019membership)

[Foundations of fully dynamic group signatures (**Journal of Cryptology 2020**)](bootle2020foundations)
