## How to Prove All NP Statements in Zero-Knowledge and a Methodology of Cryptographic Protocol Design

## 笔记

We show that every language in NP has a zero-knowledge proof, assuming the existence of one-way functions. The proof is a probability polynomial time interactive protocol, while the argument for zero-knowledge relies on the difficulty of isomorphism testing for Boolean formulae. The underlying methodology for the construction is general, providing a paradigm for secure multi-party protocols.
