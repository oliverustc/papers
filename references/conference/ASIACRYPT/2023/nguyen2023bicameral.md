## 笔记

This work introduces bicameral and auditably private signatures, a new signature primitive that provides privacy guarantees for signers. The scheme supports two main properties: bicameral signing that allows flexible delegation of signing rights, and auditable privacy that enables authorized auditors to link signatures to signers while maintaining privacy against other parties.

以下是中文翻译：

本研究介绍了双室盲签名和可审计隐私签名，一种新的签名原语，为签名者提供隐私保证。该方案支持两个主要特性：双室签名允许灵活地委托签名权利，可审计隐私性使得授权的审计人员能够将签名链接到签名者，同时保持对其他方的隐私。
